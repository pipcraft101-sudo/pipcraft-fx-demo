Scalping Strategies (1‑15 min timeframe)

Scalping

M1 Momentum Scalp

Quick entries at the 1‑minute chart based on RSI divergence and moving average retests.

Entry Rules

  • RSI crosses above 30 after being oversold
  • Price retraces to 9‑EMA on M1 chart
  • Candlestick closes above previous high

Risk Management

Stop loss at recent swing low, take profit at 2:1 RR, max 0.5% risk per trade.
